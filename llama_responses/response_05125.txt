To find the total distance that Mercedes and Davonte ran, we need to find the distance that Davonte ran and add it to the distance that Mercedes ran.

Mercedes ran 7.5 kilometers. Davonte ran 2 kilometers more than Mercedes, so Davonte ran 7.5 + 2 = 9.5 kilometers.

The total distance that Mercedes and Davonte ran is 7.5 + 9.5 = 17 kilometers.

Answer: 17
