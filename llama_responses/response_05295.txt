Let's think step by step.

Jack orders 3 sandwiches that cost $5 each.  So, the total cost of the sandwiches is 3 x $5 = $15.

Jack pays with a $20 bill.  So, he pays $20.

To find out how much he gets in change, we subtract the cost of the sandwiches from the amount he paid.  $20 - $15 = $5.

Answer: 5
