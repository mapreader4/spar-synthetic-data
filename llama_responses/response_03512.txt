Let's break this down step by step:

1. Roy goes to school 5 days a week.
2. He spends 2 hours on sports activities in school every day.
3. To find the total hours he spends on sports in school in a week, we multiply the number of hours per day by the number of days in a week: 2 hours/day * 5 days = 10 hours.
