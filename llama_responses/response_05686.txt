To solve this problem, we need to find the total amount of money Linda earned from selling the tees and jeans.

The amount of money earned from selling 7 tees is 7 x 8 = 56 dollars.
The amount of money earned from selling 4 jeans is 4 x 11 = 44 dollars.
The total amount of money earned is 56 + 44 = 100 dollars.
