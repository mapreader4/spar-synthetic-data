Let's break down the time spent cleaning the living room into smaller tasks.

1. Picking up toys: 5 minutes
2. Straightening the living room: 5 minutes
3. Vacuuming the living room floor: 20 minutes
4. Seating and draping: 15 minutes
5. Cleaning the windows: 10 minutes
6. Dusting the furniture: 10 minutes

5 + 5 + 20 + 15 + 10 + 10 = 60 minutes
