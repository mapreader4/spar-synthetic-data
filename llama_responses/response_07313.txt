To make 4 hats, James needs 1 yard of velvet. To make a cloak, James needs 3 yards of velvet. So, to make 1 cloak, James needs 1/3 of a yard of velvet. To make 6 cloaks, James needs 6/3 of a yard of velvet. To make 12 hats, James needs 12/3 of a yard of velvet. To make 6 cloaks and 12 hats, James needs 6/3 + 12/3 = 6 + 4 = 10 yards of velvet. 

Answer: 10
