To find the number of water jugs the custodian needs to fill, we need to find the total number of cups of water the students need. 200 students need 10 cups of water each, so the total number of cups needed is 200 cups. The custodian can fill 40 cups of water in each jug. So, we need to divide the total number of cups needed by the capacity of each jug. 200 cups / 40 cups per jug = 5 jugs.
