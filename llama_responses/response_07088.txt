Let's think step by step.

The base cost of the sundae is $2.00. Each topping costs $0.50. Since there are 10 toppings, the total cost of the toppings is 10 x $0.50 = $5.00. The total cost of the sundae with toppings is the base cost plus the cost of the toppings, so it is $2.00 + $5.00 = $7.00.
