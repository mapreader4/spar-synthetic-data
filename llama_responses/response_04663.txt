To find the total cost of gas, we can multiply the cost per liter of gas by the total number of liters of gas. Since Mr. Deane is getting 10 liters of gas today, and then another 25 liters on Friday, we can calculate the total number of liters of gas as 10 + 25 = 35.

The cost per liter of gas is $1.4. So, the total cost of gas will be 1.4 * 35 = 49. The answer is:

Answer: 49
