To find the total number of toothbrushes given in a 5 day work week, we can multiply the number of toothbrushes given per day by the number of days in a week.

The number of toothbrushes given per day is 2, and the number of days in a week is 5. So the total number of toothbrushes given in a 5 day work week is:

2 * 5 = 10

Answer: 10
